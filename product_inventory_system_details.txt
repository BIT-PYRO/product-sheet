- Serializers: many model serializers providing tenant/company read-only fields, custom create/update behaviors (e.g., PicklistGroup bulk create, StoneStockEntry updates StoneItem qty/weight)
- Views / APIs (ViewSets):
  - Inventory transactions CRUD (default router '')
  - Picklist groups CRUD: `picklist-groups`
  - Stone items: `stone-items` with extra actions:

  - Stone stock entries: `stone-stock-entries` (add stock)
  - Product inventory: `product-inventory` with `POST /product-inventory/bulk-upload/` for bulk item creation from array of rows

- Features:

**Additional Codebase Details**

A. Admin registrations
- `backend/products/admin.py`: registers `Collection`, `Material`, `Category`, `Channel`, `TableColumnConfig`, `Product` with admin list displays, search_fields and list_filters for quick admin lookup.

B. Services (key helpers and sync logic)
- `backend/products/services/product_service.py`: `calculate_margin(selling_price, cost_price)` — simple margin utility returning percentage as Decimal.
- `backend/inventory/services/stone_sync.py`:
  - `sync_stones_from_sheets()` — scans `Product.stone_entries` and `DesignerSheet.stone_entries` to create/update `StoneItem` catalog records (catalog-only, never overwrites qty fields). Returns summary `{created, updated, skipped}`.
  - `sync_stone_to_sheets(stone_item)` — pushes catalog field updates from a `StoneItem` into matching `Product` and `DesignerSheet` entries by fingerprint.
  - Fingerprint used: tuple of normalized descriptor fields `(type, variety, color, cut, shape, length, width, height)`.
- `backend/inventory/services/die_sync.py`:
  - `sync_all_dies_from_sheets()` — reconciles `DieInventoryItem` records from `DesignerSheet.tracking_rows` and `Product.die_numbers` and synchronizes `designer_skus` / `master_skus` and merged design images.
  - Utility `_parse_die_legacy_value` to extract die codes from legacy formatted strings.
- `backend/inventory/services/inventory_service.py`:
  - `create_stock_transaction(product=..., txn_type, quantity, remark)` — atomic helper to create `InventoryTransaction` rows with sanity checks.

C. Signals
- `backend/inventory/signals.py`:
  - `connect_signals()` wires `post_save` handlers for `DesignerSheet` and `Product` that trigger a full die sync via `sync_all_dies_from_sheets`. Handlers swallow exceptions to avoid blocking saves.

D. Management commands & extras
- `backend/products/management/commands/fix_die_legacy_values.py` — an example management command for data-fixing (search other management commands for maintenance scripts).

- Many write paths wrap critical operations in `transaction.atomic()` to ensure consistency (picklist bulk create, stone sync, die sync, issue-request reviews).
- Tenancy and per-company scoping is enforced using `core_tenants.TenantCompanyModel` and `TenantAwareModel` base classes — most models include `tenant` and `company` scoping fields.
